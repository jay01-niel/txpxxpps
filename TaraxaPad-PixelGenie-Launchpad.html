Project Summary

PixelGenie is an NFT platform designed to be a digital marketplace that'll enable users discover, trade, and create NFTs native to the Taraxa blockDag. Additionally, our mission is to provide collection owners with an array of tools to design, create, and offer unique utilities to their communities, all while eliminating the technical complexities that often hinder aspiring creators.

Project Overview

PixelGenie aims to enhance the NFT ecosystem by offering a comprehensive suite of tools and features for creators and collectors alike. Here are the outstanding features of PixelGenie:

Features

  • Effortless NFT Creation: Transform your media into NFTs at a low cost. Convert JPEGs to NFTs in seconds, complete with metadata support.
  • Seamless Collection Management: Easily create and manage your own NFT collection through the "Create My NFT" section on PixelGenie.
  • Flexible Purchasing Options: Acquire listed NFTs by either (1) buying at a fixed price or (2) making an offer on an NFT.
  • Versatile Marketplace: Sell or auction any NFTs you own, not just those obtained through PixelGenie. Taraxa's low gas costs facilitate the sale of lower-priced NFTs.
  • Comprehensive Collection Viewing: Access all listed NFT collections from the Homepage or Collections page.
  • Quality NFTs: PixelGenie offers users the opportunity to buy and trade high-quality NFTs, ensuring an exceptional experience for creators and collectors.
  • Liquidity Provision: Provide liquidity for your project in our NFT pool by creating an NFT staking pool to incentivize your holders.
  • On-Chain Royalty Support: Earn royalties on every sale of your minted NFTs on PixelGenie, with full on-chain support.
  • Transaction Flexibility: Conduct transactions using TARA or our official token ($GENIE) on the PixelGenie marketplace.
  • Collateralized Lending: Use your NFTs as collateral to borrow $GENIE or TARA, leveraging the value of your digital assets.

Roadmap

Welcome to the PixelGenie Roadmap, a summarized version of our whitepaper outlining our journey to becoming a premier NFT marketplace. For a more detailed roadmap, please refer to our whitepaper. It contains details of our phased approach to development, from our initial vision and foundational features to expansive growth and advanced utilities.

Phase 1: The Start

  • Establish PixelGenie as an NFT marketplace on Taraxa, focused on user-centric design.
  • Outline essential functionalities including NFT creation, buying, bidding, selling, and governance utilizing the $GENIE token.
  • Release the comprehensive PixelGenie whitepaper, detailing marketplace features, governance structure, and technical specifications.
  • Provide in-depth information on our platform's features and projected milestones.
  • Partner with projects on Taraxa to bolster our platform’s reach and capabilities.
  • Present a first look at the marketplace, showcasing its unique features and functionalities to the PixelGenie community.
  • Begin the presale of the $GENIE token.
  • Launch the $GENIE token following the completion of the presale.

    • Phase 2: Feature Expansion

  • Continuously refine the user interface to ensure seamless and intuitive interactions.
  • Integrate user-friendly tools for NFT creation, buying, and selling.
  • Officially launch the PixelGenie marketplace on the Taraxa Mainnet.
  • Enable comprehensive functionality for NFT creation, listing, bidding, and transactions.
  • Introduce a launchpad feature to facilitate the release of new NFT projects via our exclusive NFT minting dApp.
  • Implement $GENIE staking and farming mechanisms to incentivize holders.
  • Gather user feedback to drive ongoing platform enhancements and ensure alignment with user needs and technological advancements.

    • Phase 3: Utility and Ecosystem Growth

  • Introduce new utilities for the $GENIE token to increase its value and functionality within the ecosystem.
  • Launch the PixelGenie NFT lending and borrowing platform, providing new financial opportunities for users.
  • Fully deploy the governance model, empowering $GENIE holders to influence platform decisions.
  • Enable proposals and voting on critical platform developments and policies through GenieDAO.
  • Develop and deploy additional tools and services for creators, enhancing their ability to produce and manage high-quality NFTs.
  • Maintain an continuous commitment to platform optimization, leveraging user feedback and technological advancements to ensure PixelGenie remains at the forefront of the NFT marketplace on Taraxa.

    • This roadmap outlines a clear, strategic path for PixelGenie, ensuring the platform's evolution from inception to a fully realized, community-driven NFT marketplace with a robust ecosystem and advanced user features.
